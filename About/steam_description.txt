[h1]RimSynapse - Psychology[/h1]
[b]Brings your colonists to life by giving them evolving memories, complex interpersonal trust, therapeutic sessions, and trauma responses using LLMs.[/b]

Breathe genuine psychological depth into your colonists. RimSynapse Psychology replaces rigid vanilla opinions with a fluid Trust/Familiarity system and lets pawns accumulate core memories that dynamically reshape their traits and mental breaks based on LLM analysis.

---

[h2]Key Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Dynamic Personality Traits:[/b] The 24-hour psychological evaluation tracks profound life events. Pawns can dynamically gain or lose RimWorld traits (such as gaining Bloodlust after a brutal raid, or losing Kind) based on LLM analysis of their rolling short-term memory.
[*] [b]Trust & Familiarity System:[/b] Replaces vanilla's rigid opinion system with dynamic Trust (-100 to 100) and Familiarity (0 to 100) values. Vanilla OpinionOf is scaled down by 50%, with the custom Trust metric contributing the remaining 50% weight.
[*] [b]PTSD & Suicidal Breaks:[/b] Pawns suffering severe trauma can experience extreme mental breaks: they may enter a "Trauma Trigger" break (blindly firing at doors, walls, or loud noises before cowering) or attempt a lethal "Suicide/Self-Harm" break using their equipped primary weapon to target critical organs with a 5x damage multiplier.
[*] [b]Therapy & Consoling:[/b] Right-click target pawns to initiate a Therapy Session. The therapist and patient will sit together and dynamically converse via the background LLM engine. Successful sessions have a dynamic chance (influenced by mood) to permanently [b]cure/remove severe negative and psychological traits[/b] (including PTSD, Depressive, Volatile, Nervous, Psychopath, and Bloodlust - unless locked by their backstory).
[*] [b]Opportunistic Euphoria:[/b] Pawns who maintain an extremely high mood (>90%) for a sustained 24 in-game hours will automatically generate a positive core memory.
[*] [b]Ritual Remarks:[/b] Colonists pull from their LLM relationship memories to deliver personalized Vows during marriages and Eulogies during funerals.
[*] [b]Forced Psychological Reviews:[/b] Demand an immediate clinical evaluation of the pawn's mental state from the LLM via the new Social Network tab.
[*] [b]World VIP Backstories:[/b] Faction Leaders generate with a rich, ideologically consistent history and personality profile.
[/list]
