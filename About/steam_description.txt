[h1]RimSynapse - Psychology[/h1]
[b]📚 [url=https://github.com/RimSynapse/RimSynapse-Psychology/wiki]Official Wiki &amp; Documentation[/url][/b]

[b]Companion Mod for RimSynapse. Requires RimSynapse - Core.[/b]
Brings your colonists to life by giving them evolving memories, complex interpersonal trust, therapeutic sessions, and trauma responses. 

[h2]Current Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Dynamic Personality Traits:[/b] The 24-hour psychological evaluation tracks profound life events. Pawns can dynamically gain or lose RimWorld traits (such as gaining [i]Bloodlust[/i] after a brutal raid, or losing [i]Kind[/i]) based on LLM analysis of their rolling short-term memory.
[*] [b]Trust &amp; Familiarity System:[/b] Replaces vanilla's rigid opinion system with dynamic Trust (-100 to 100) and Familiarity (0 to 100) values. Vanilla [i]OpinionOf[/i] is scaled down by 50%, with the custom Trust metric contributing the remaining 50% weight.
[*] [b]PTSD &amp; Trauma Breaks:[/b] A new mental state, [i]Trauma Trigger[/i], causes pawns with severe negative memories to blindly fire their weapon at surrounding doors, walls, or loud noises before cowering in fear. Hitting a living creature snaps them out of it.
[*] [b]Player-Driven Therapy:[/b] Right-click pawns to initiate a Therapy Session. Act as a "Guiding Hand" and manually type out the initiating pawn's dialogue, or let the LLM resolve the conversation in the background based on their Intelligence, Social skill, and Ideology.
[*] [b]Opportunistic Euphoria:[/b] Pawns who maintain an extremely high mood (>90%) for a sustained 24 in-game hours will automatically generate a positive core memory.
[*] [b]Ritual Remarks:[/b] Colonists pull from their LLM relationship memories to deliver personalized Vows during marriages and Eulogies during funerals.
[*] [b]Forced Psychological Reviews:[/b] Demand an immediate clinical evaluation of the pawn's mental state from the LLM via the new Social Network tab.
[*] [b]World VIP Backstories:[/b] Faction Leaders are no longer generic. They generate with a rich, ideologically consistent history and personality profile.
[/list]

[h2]Planned Upcoming Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Couples Counseling &amp; Dynamic Romance:[/b] Breakups and romances driven entirely by shared memories and psychological compatibility rather than RNG.
[*] [b]LLM-Generated Phobias:[/b] Pawns who suffer specific traumas (e.g., severe burns, insectoid attacks) may develop targeted phobias that alter their pathing and combat behavior.
[*] [b]Group Therapy:[/b] Let your highest-social pawn lead group sessions to mass-resolve colony-wide trauma after devastating events.
[*] [b]Psychological Addictions:[/b] Pawns may turn to specific drugs as a coping mechanism for specific negative memories.
[/list]

[*] [b]Anomaly DLC:[/b] Compatible and Optional. Integrates void taint and cosmic horror PTSD.
